QC Checklist

  • ? Verify name matches passport/ID and title (MR/MRS/MS) is correct.
  • ? Confirm all flight segments, dates, and times match the booking request.
  • ? Ensure the correct fare basis is used and ticket rules are noted.
  • ? Check that all applicable taxes and carrier-imposed fees are correct.
  • ? Verify the FOP is correct (e.g., credit card last 4 digits, cash, invoice).
  • ? Ensure the original ticket is cross-referenced in the endorsement box.
  • ? Verify the correct change fee was applied and collected, if applicable.
  • ? Confirm any fare difference has been accurately calculated and collected/refunded.
  • ? Check the fare construction line to ensure it is accurate for the new itinerary.
  • ? Update the endorsement box with any new restrictions (e.g., NON-REF/CHG FEE).
  • ? Confirm the refund has been processed in the GDS or via the airline's portal.
  • ? Ensure the correct cancellation penalty was deducted from the refund amount.
  • ? Verify that any non-refundable taxes have been correctly handled.
  • ? Double-check the final refund amount against fare rules and calculations.
  • ? Confirm the refund was sent back to the original form of payment.
